Cheese Stick

Ingredients

  • 7 ounces cheddar cheese sliced into individual strips
  • 30 pieces spring roll lumpia wrapper
  • 1 cup cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Wrap the cheeses in spring roll wrapper.
  2. Heat the pan and pour-in the cooking oil. Deep-fry the cheese in spring roll wrapper for 4 minutes or until the color of the spring roll wrapper turns golden brown.
  3. Place in a plate with paper towel.
  4. Serve with mayonnaise and ketchup.
